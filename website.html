AboutUs

            Interestingly, Kayum was never the real owner of the brand Barbeque Nation.
             The story goes back to 2006, when his brother Sajid Dhanani, who ran the 
             well-known Sayaji Hotels in Indore, once came across customers complaining 
             of cold appetizers, during winters.
             

             Barbeque-Nation was originally incorporated as Sanchi Hotels Private Limited 
             on October 13 2006 at Indore Madhya Pradesh as a private limited company. In
              2008, the name of the Company was changed to Barbeque-Nation Hospitality 
              Private Limited and moved its headquarters to Bengaluru in 2014.
              

              The promoters of Barbeque Nation Hospitality are Sayaji Hotels, Sayaji 
              Housekeeping Services, Kayum Dhanani, Raoof Dhanani and Suchitra Dhanani.
               Promoter and promoter group's shareholding stood at 47.80 percent and the
                rest is held by public shareholders.
